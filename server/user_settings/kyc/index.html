KYC verification

Identity checks now have a dedicated location in the account surface instead of a dead sidebar link.

Step 1

Government-issued ID

Upload a valid passport, driver license, or national ID.

Step 2

Proof of address

Provide a recent utility bill or bank statement dated within the last 90 days.

Step 3

Compliance review

Once approved, larger withdrawals and higher allocation limits become available.
